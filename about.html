Our Story

AND OUR MISSION TO PROTECT LIP BALM

My name is Stephanie Richardson, I as well as others tend to suffer from melted lip balm exposed to the heat. I tend to use lip balm on a daily basis, I wish there was some way I could have less worries on melted lip balm. I then started researching and experimenting with different covers to use on your chapstick. None of these tend to work so I then thought about using the same material “hydroflasks” use to maintain temperature inside their container over time. Gelus Lips has designed a double wall vacuum insulated container specifically to fit your lip balm.

Vacuum insulated and molded with stainless steel, the GeliCase provides the most premium experience for carrying your lip balm. We are here to preserve temperature and protect your lip balm in style.
